Outcome is the venue and primitive composer for **HIP-4** on **Hyperliquid L1**. At face value, prediction markets - every outcome token is a contract that pays exactly **$1.00 USDH** if its outcome wins. Underneath, a modular stack of financial primitives that compose into structured products and synthetic exposure that DeFi can't natively express elsewhere. Same on-chain orderbook that powers Hyperliquid perps, same fast finality, same agent-key auth model. No oracles between you and the price.

## What's on Outcome

- **Outcome tokens** - Yes and No shares of every outcome, priced 0–1 as the implied probability, settled in USDH.
- **Four market types** - recurring price binary, labelled binary, multi-outcome, and price bucket. See [Market types](/markets/types).
- **Four conversion primitives** - split, merge outcome, merge question, negate. The protocol mints, burns, and rotates bundles directly against your spot balance, with no orderbook spread paid. See [Composition stack](/concepts/composition-stack).
- **Hyperliquid-native** - agent-key signing, L1 finality, no gas on trades, the same orderbook engine you already know.
