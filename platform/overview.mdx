Outcome is a trading interface for HIP-4 prediction markets built on Hyperliquid L1. You trade probability — not price charts — by buying and selling outcome tokens that represent your view on whether a specific event will happen. Every market resolves to a clear winner, and winning tokens pay out exactly $1.00 each in USDH.

## What are prediction markets?

Prediction markets let you trade on the probability of real-world events. Each market has two or more possible outcomes (for example, Yes and No), and the tokens for each outcome are priced between 0 and 1 — where the price represents the market's collective estimate of that outcome's probability.

A Yes token priced at 0.65 means the market currently implies a 65% chance the event happens. If you agree, you buy Yes tokens. If you think the market is overestimating the probability, you buy No tokens instead. When the market resolves, the winning outcome's tokens are each redeemable for $1.00 USDH, and the losing tokens expire worthless.

## What you can trade

Outcome supports three types of markets:

- **Recurring price binary markets** — automatically generated markets that ask whether an asset (like BTC) will be above or below a specific price at a specific time, such as "BTC > $69,000 by 3pm." New versions are created on a recurring schedule.
- **Named outcome markets** — custom binary markets on events like elections, sports, or protocol decisions, with side names reflecting the actual choices (for example, "Hypurr" vs. "Usain Bolt").
- **Multi-outcome events** — grouped markets under a single question where multiple named outcomes compete, with one winner taking the $1.00 payout.

## How markets resolve

When a market reaches its expiry, trading stops and resolution begins. The platform determines the winning outcome based on the resolution criteria (for price binary markets, this is the oracle price at expiry). Winning tokens become redeemable for $1.00 USDH each. Losing tokens expire worthless with no payout.

## Collateral: USDC → USDH

All prediction market trading on Outcome uses USDH — the platform's prediction market collateral token on Hyperliquid. You fund your account by depositing USDC and converting it to USDH through the platform's deposit flow. USDH is acquired at approximately the USDC oracle price and is used exclusively within Hyperliquid prediction markets.

## Network: Hyperliquid L1

Outcome runs on Hyperliquid L1, which means your orders settle with fast finality and you pay no gas fees on trades. The orderbook infrastructure is the same high-performance exchange that powers Hyperliquid perpetuals.
