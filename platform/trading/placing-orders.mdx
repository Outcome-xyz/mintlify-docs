Placing a trade on Outcome means buying or selling outcome tokens on a specific market. You choose your side (Yes or No, or a named outcome), pick an order type, set your size, and submit. Your order goes directly to the Hyperliquid L1 orderbook — there's no intermediate step, and fills are near-instant.

## Before you start

Make sure you have:

- A connected wallet (MetaMask, WalletConnect, or another supported wallet)
- USDH in your account — see [Depositing funds](/platform/funds/depositing) if you haven't funded yet

## Placing a trade

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your wallet">
    Click **Connect Wallet** in the top right corner and follow the prompts for your wallet provider (MetaMask, WalletConnect, or another supported option). Once connected, your address and USDH balance will appear in the header.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to a market">
    Browse the markets list and click the market you want to trade. You'll see the current token prices, orderbook depth, and recent trade history for that market.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your side">
    Select **Yes** or **No** (or the named outcome you want to back). The trading panel will update to show the current best price for that side.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your order type">
    Choose between **Limit** and **Market**:
    - **Limit** — you set the exact price you're willing to pay. Your order rests on the book until someone fills it.
    - **Market** — your order executes immediately at the best available price.

    See [Order types](/platform/trading/order-types) for a full comparison.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your amount in shares">
    Type the number of tokens (shares) you want to buy or sell. Each share represents one outcome token. If you win, each share pays $1.00 USDH.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your price (limit orders only)">
    For limit orders, enter the price you want to pay per share, between **0.00** and **1.00**. This is also the probability you're implying for that outcome. For example, entering **0.60** means you're paying 60¢ per share and implying a 60% chance of winning.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review your cost">
    The platform shows your estimated cost before you submit:

    **Cost = price × shares (in USDH)**

    For example, 100 shares at $0.60 each = $60.00 USDH. Make sure your USDH balance covers this amount.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit and sign">
    Click **Place Order** and approve the transaction in your wallet. Your wallet will prompt you to sign an EIP-712 message — this authorizes the trade without sending funds through a smart contract. The order is submitted to the Hyperliquid L1 orderbook immediately after signing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Minimum order size

The minimum order size is **$10 USDH notional**. This means your price × shares must equal at least $10.00. For example:

- 100 shares at $0.10 each = $10.00 — meets the minimum
- 50 shares at $0.10 each = $5.00 — below the minimum, order will be rejected

The platform will show an error before submission if your order is below the minimum.

## What happens after you submit

Your order goes directly to the Hyperliquid L1 orderbook:

- **Limit orders** rest on the book until someone fills them (or until you cancel).
- **Market orders** execute immediately against the best available resting orders.

Once filled, your tokens appear in the **Positions** tab. You can monitor your open positions, average entry price, current value, and potential payout there.

<Tip>
  Limit orders usually get better prices than market orders. Set your limit price close to the current mid-price for faster fills while still avoiding slippage.
</Tip>

<Warning>
  Market orders fill at whatever price is available in the orderbook. In thin markets, this can mean significantly worse prices than the displayed mid-price. Check the orderbook depth before placing a large market order.
</Warning>
