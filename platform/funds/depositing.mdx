USDH is the underlying asset for every existing HIP-4 market. To trade, you need USDH on the **HyperCore spot side** of your Hyperliquid account. Outcome's funding flow handles every step of getting you there - bridge in, transfer to spot, buy USDH - through two integrated providers.

## Sign in first

Outcome auth runs through **Privy**. Two paths today:

- **Wallet** - connect MetaMask, Rabby, or any EVM wallet via WalletConnect.
- **Email or SSO** - Privy provisions a wallet for you under the hood. No extension to install, no seed phrase to manage upfront. Use email-OTP or any of Privy's SSO providers.

Smart-wallet support (account abstraction, gasless flows, batched transactions) is coming.

## Two ways to deposit

| Provider   | What it handles | Source                                                                                                                         |
| ---------- | --------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Across** | Crypto deposits | Bridge USDC (or supported assets) from Ethereum, Arbitrum, Base, Optimism, Polygon, and other supported chains to Hyperliquid. |
| **Meld**   | Cash deposits   | Buy USDC with card, bank transfer, Apple Pay, and other fiat methods.                                                          |

Either path arrives at the same endpoint: USDH in your spot account, ready to trade HIP-4 markets.

## The flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    Connect a wallet, or sign in with email / SSO via Privy.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a deposit method">
    In the Wallet section, choose **Crypto** (Across) or **Cash** (Meld).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send funds">
    For crypto: pick the source chain and asset, sign a bridge transaction with your wallet. Across handles the cross-chain delivery to your Hyperliquid account.

    For cash: enter the amount and payment method, complete the Meld checkout flow.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Outcome converts to USDH automatically">
    Funds land on Hyperliquid. Outcome moves them across to HyperCore spot and buys USDH on your behalf. No manual transfer-to-spot step, no manual buy-USDH step.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Trade">
    USDH balance shows up in your Wallet tab. Place orders against any HIP-4 market.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Why USDH

USDH is the collateral token for every existing HIP-4 market. Buying or selling outcome tokens means moving USDH in and out of your spot balance. You can't trade HIP-4 directly with USDC - you need USDH.

USDH ≈ USDC at the oracle price. The peg is very tight in practice, and HIP-4 markets settle in USDH at exactly **1 USDH per winning share**, independent of any short-term USDC drift.

## Minimums and fees

- **Outcome** doesn't charge a deposit fee. The only fees you pay are the provider's (Across' bridge fee or Meld's fiat-processing fee) and Hyperliquid's standard maker/taker on the spot leg of the USDH purchase.
- **Each provider has its own minimums** - Across has a gas-economic floor, Meld has fiat-amount thresholds.
- **HIP-4 orders require $10 minimum notional**, so deposit at least enough to make multiple orders comfortably.

## What's happening under the hood

Hyperliquid is dual-chain:

- **HyperCore** - the L1 with the on-chain orderbook. Spot trading and HIP-4 outcomes live here.
- **HyperEVM** - the EVM-compatible chain. Perps and HIP-3 live here.

A USDC deposit from an external chain doesn't automatically land on HyperCore spot - it lands on the perps/EVM side and needs to be transferred. Outcome's Across + Meld integration absorbs all of that. From the user's perspective, you deposit and you have USDH; the chain plumbing happens behind the scenes.
