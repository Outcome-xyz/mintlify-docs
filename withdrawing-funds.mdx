---
title: "Withdrawing funds"
---

You can withdraw your USDH balance from Outcome at any time. Withdrawals happen in two steps: your USDH is converted to USDC on Hyperliquid, then bridged out via Hyperliquid's native bridge to an EVM address on Arbitrum.

### **How to withdraw**

Open the Withdraw flow in your [<u>outcome.xyz</u>](http://outcome.xyz) profile page, enter the amount, choose the destination address, and confirm. Outcome walks you through the USDH-to-USDC conversion and the bridge step. The minimum withdrawal amount is 10 USDH.

### **Destination**

Withdrawals land on Arbitrum. You can send them to:

- Your connected wallet's address (the default).
- Any other EVM address you specify.

Double-check the destination before confirming. Crypto transactions cannot be reversed if you send to the wrong address.

### **Fees and timing**

**Fees.** Hyperliquid charges a flat fee on the bridge withdrawal. The fee is shown before you confirm.

**Timing.** Withdrawals typically complete within a few minutes once both steps are submitted.
