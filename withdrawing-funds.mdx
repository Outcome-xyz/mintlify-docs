You can withdraw your USDC balance from Outcome at any time. Your USDC is bridged out via Circle's CCTP (Cross-Chain Transfer Protocol) to an EVM address on Arbitrum.

### **How to withdraw**

Open the Withdraw flow in your [<u>outcome.xyz</u>](http://outcome.xyz) profile page, enter the amount, choose the destination address, and confirm. Outcome walks you through the CCTP bridge step. The minimum withdrawal amount is 2 USDC.

### **Destination**

Withdrawals land on Arbitrum. You can send them to:

- Your connected wallet's address (the default).
- Any other EVM address you specify.

Double-check the destination before confirming. Crypto transactions cannot be reversed if you send to the wrong address.

### **Fees and timing**

**Fees.** Two fees apply, both shown before you confirm: a small HyperEVM gas fee (a fraction of a cent) and Circle's CCTP forwarding fee, which varies by destination chain (roughly 20 cents to Arbitrum, more to chains like Ethereum). Outcome charges no withdrawal fee of its own.

**Timing.** Withdrawals typically complete within seconds once submitted.
