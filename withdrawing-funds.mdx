You can withdraw your USDC balance from Outcome at any time. There are two ways out: bridge USDC to another chain, or cash out to fiat.

### Withdraw USDC to Arbitrum

USDC withdrawals are powered by Circle's Cross-Chain Transfer Protocol (CCTP), which moves native USDC across chains. Open the Withdraw flow in [outcome.xyz](http://outcome.xyz), enter the amount, choose the destination chain (currently set as Arbitrum by default) and address, and confirm.

You can send to your connected wallet's address or any other EVM address you specify. Double-check the destination before confirming. Crypto transactions cannot be reversed if you send to the wrong address.

### Withdraw to fiat (coming soon)

If you want to cash out to fiat currency, withdrawals are powered by [Meld](https://meld.io/), a crypto off-ramp aggregator. Meld converts your USDC to fiat and sends it to your chosen payment method. This requires identity verification (KYC). Availability, supported currencies, and payment methods depend on your region.

### Fees and timing

**Fees.** USDC withdrawals via CCTP incur network costs, shown before you confirm. Fiat withdrawals via Meld incur Meld's fees, which depend on the payment method and region.

**Timing.** USDC withdrawals typically complete within a few minutes. Fiat withdrawals depend on the payment rail and may take longer.
