To start trading on Outcome, you'll connect a wallet, fund your account, and place an order. If you already trade on Hyperliquid, your existing wallet and balance work here.

## Connect a wallet

Visit [outcome.xyz](https://outcome.xyz) and click **Connect**. Outcome supports popular EVM wallets like MetaMask and Rabby.

The first time you connect, you'll sign a message to create your account. This costs nothing and doesn't deploy anything on-chain.

## Fund your account

**Deposit from another chain.** Outcome has a built-in bridge that lets you fund your account from another chain without leaving the app. The bridge is powered by [Across](https://across.to) and automatically converts your deposit to USDH on arrival, so you don't need to acquire USDH ahead of time.

**Convert from Hyperliquid USDC.** If you already hold USDC on Hyperliquid, no bridging is needed. When you connect your wallet, Outcome detects the USDC balance and offers in-app conversion to USDH.

Once funded, your USDH balance is ready to trade.

## Place your first trade

Before your first order, you'll enable trading by signing a one-time message. This lets you place orders without signing each one individually, giving you a single-signature experience similar to a centralized exchange while your funds stay non-custodial and on-chain.

Once trading is enabled, open a market and pick the outcome you want to trade. Enter the price and the size of your position, then confirm. Once your order is filled, the position appears in your portfolio.

## Closing a position

You don't have to wait for settlement. To exit before the market resolves, sell your tokens on the order book at the current price. The proceeds return to your USDH balance.

If you hold to settlement, your position settles automatically based on how the market resolves. Tokens for the resolved outcome pay 1 USDH each, and tokens for any other outcome pay 0. Your USDH balance updates automatically, with no action required.
