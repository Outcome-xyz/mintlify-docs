---
title: "Trading fee discount"
---

When you sign up for Outcome, you have the option to activate a referral code on your Hyperliquid account, giving you a 4% discount on Hyperliquid trading fees.

### **What this means in practice**

The discount applies to the taker fees you'd otherwise pay on Hyperliquid, including the taker fees when closing positions on Outcome. It stacks with other fee adjustments, like the USDH aligned-quote benefit. See Fees for how the full fee schedule works.

### **How to activate it**

The option is presented during your first sign-up. If you skip it then, you can activate it later from your profile page on [outcome.xyz](http://outcome.xyz). Once activated, the discount applies to your Hyperliquid trading fees going forward.
