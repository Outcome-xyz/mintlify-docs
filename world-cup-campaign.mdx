From the opening match on June 11 through the final on July 19, Outcome is rewarding trading and liquidity across its World Cup markets. Rewards are paid in USD, and only orders and trades placed on [outcome.xyz](http://outcome.xyz) are eligible.

### The two programs

**The champion market.** "2026 World Cup champion?" runs for the whole tournament. Every day there is a \$1,000 reward pool, shared across everyone providing liquidity and trading on it. As teams are knocked out, the rewards naturally shift toward the teams still in contention.

**The match markets.** Every match has its own reward pool, and the pools grow as the tournament progresses:

| Stage | Reward pool per match |
| :-- | :-- |
| Group stage | \$100 |
| Round of 32 | \$200 |
| Round of 16 | \$400 |
| Quarter finals | \$600 |
| Semi finals | \$1,000 |
| Third place | \$600 |
| Final | \$2,000 |

### The live match bonus

Provide liquidity or trade on a match while it is being played, and your rewards on that match are tripled. Markets move fastest during live play, which is exactly when they need liquidity most, so that is when the program pays the most for it.

### How you earn

You can earn two ways, and you can do both:

- **Limit orders posted near the current price, on both sides (a buy and a sell), earn the most.** You are rewarded for showing depth, and rewarded again when your orders get filled.
- **Market orders earn a share too.**

The closer your orders sit to the price, and the more you keep both a buy and a sell up, the more you earn.

Each pool is shared proportionally, so the more useful your liquidity is compared to everyone else's, the larger your share. There are no fixed per-person caps.

### What earning looks like

Say you keep a two-sided quote up on a quarter final, a buy and a sell, both close to the current price. You earn from the rewards the whole time those orders are live. When someone trades against them, you earn again. Keep them up once the match kicks off, and everything you earn on that match is tripled.

### Eligible markets

Each morning at 09:00 UTC, the markets eligible for rewards that day are published. Check the day's list before quoting.

### Key details

- Runs from the opening match on June 11 through the final on July 19.
- Eligible markets are published every morning at 09:00 UTC.
- Rewards are paid in USD.
- Only orders and trades placed on [outcome.xyz](http://outcome.xyz) count.
- The infrastructure behind the rewards program is powered by Monarch.\
